# Warranties Policy

## Standard Policy
Warranty coverage depends on product category, manufacturer terms, purchase date, and evidence of defect. Scout may summarize warranty guidance only from verified product or policy data.

## Requirements
- Proof of purchase or order identifier.
- Product identifier or exact product name.
- Description of the defect and when it appeared.

## Exceptions
- Normal wear, misuse, accidental damage, unauthorized repair, and consumable parts may be excluded.
- Damage on arrival follows `damaged_items.md` before warranty review.

## Cross-Policy Notes
If warranty coverage is unavailable and the item is still return-eligible, use `returns.md`.

Related policy files: `damaged_items.md`, `returns.md`.
