# Shipping and Delivery Policy

## Standard Policy
Delivery estimates are configured service estimates, not carrier guarantees. A delivery promise may be shown only when inventory and delivery eligibility are verified by Scout services.

## Delivery Windows
Standard prototype delivery is estimated at 3–5 days when the store network has available inventory. Expedited delivery, if offered, must show its own verified service level and cost.

## Exceptions
- Weather, carrier disruption, address validation failure, payment review, and inventory changes may alter delivery timing.
- Missing delivery claims follow `missing_packages.md`.
- Damaged delivery claims follow `damaged_items.md`.

## Cross-Policy Notes
Cancellation after shipment is limited by `order_cancellation.md`. Refunds for shipping charges are governed by `refunds.md`.
