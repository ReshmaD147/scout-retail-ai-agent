# Exchanges Policy

## Standard Policy
Eligible items may be exchanged within the same 30-day return window when the replacement item is available and the original item meets return requirements.

## Exchange Rules
- The replacement must be a catalog product currently available for sale.
- Price differences must be disclosed before the exchange is completed.
- Inventory for pickup or delivery must be verified before promising fulfillment.

## Exceptions
- Final-sale, personalized, opened hygiene, and redeemed gift-card products are not exchangeable.
- Damaged or defective exchanges require evidence under `damaged_items.md`.

## Cross-Policy Notes
If the customer wants money back instead of a replacement, use `refunds.md`. Fulfillment timing follows `shipping_delivery.md` or `store_pickup.md`.
