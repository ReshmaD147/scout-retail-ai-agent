# Returns Policy

## Standard Policy
Most eligible physical merchandise may be returned within 30 days of delivery or pickup. Eligible online purchases may be returned in store when the order can be verified and the item is not restricted to mail-back handling. Items must include the original packaging and accessories when practical. Scout may require the order number, receipt, or account session used for purchase.

## Required Evidence
- Order identifier or receipt.
- Product condition and reason for return.
- Confirmation that the item is not in a final-sale or non-returnable category.

## Exceptions
- Final-sale merchandise, personalized goods, opened hygiene items such as opened moisturizer, and redeemed gift cards are not returnable.
- Damaged, defective, or wrong-item returns follow `damaged_items.md` first.
- Missing package claims follow `missing_packages.md` first.

## Cross-Policy Notes
Refund timing and method are governed by `refunds.md`. Exchanges are governed by `exchanges.md`. Gift card returns are governed by `gift_cards.md`.
