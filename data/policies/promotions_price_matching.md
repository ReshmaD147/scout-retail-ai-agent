# Promotions and Price Matching Policy

## Standard Policy
Promotions may be displayed only when the promotion exists, applies to the product, is active for the current date, satisfies eligibility conditions, and passes verification. React must not calculate or invent promotional prices.

## Price Matching
Scout may describe price matching only when a documented active policy, eligible competitor, matching product identity, and current price evidence are available. Price adjustments must use verified backend calculations.

## Exceptions
- Expired, future, personalized, coupon-only, marketplace, clearance, and bundle-only offers may be excluded.
- Gift card discounts and stored-value adjustments follow `gift_cards.md`.

## Cross-Policy Notes
Refund-like price adjustments are not standard returns and should not bypass `refunds.md`.

Related policy files: `refunds.md`, `gift_cards.md`.
