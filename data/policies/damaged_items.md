# Damaged Items Policy

## Standard Policy
Customers should report damaged, defective, or wrong items as soon as possible. Scout must collect order evidence and product details before recommending return, refund, or exchange options.

## Required Evidence
- Order identifier or receipt.
- Affected product and quantity.
- Description of damage, defect, or wrong item.
- Photos may be required by support before final approval.

## Exceptions
- Carrier-visible package damage may require carrier documentation.
- Manufacturer defects after normal use may fall under `warranties.md`.

## Cross-Policy Notes
Approved refunds follow `refunds.md`. Replacement options follow `exchanges.md`. Delivery-related timing follows `shipping_delivery.md`.

Related policy files: `returns.md`, `refunds.md`, `exchanges.md`, `shipping_delivery.md`, `warranties.md`.
