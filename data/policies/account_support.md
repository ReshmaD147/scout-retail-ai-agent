# Account Support Policy

## Standard Policy
Account support covers sign-in help, contact detail updates, communication preferences, and customer identity verification. Scout must not expose sensitive account or order data without verified session ownership.

## Requirements
- Verify the active customer session before discussing personal account information.
- Use support-safe summaries instead of exposing sensitive internal records.
- Direct payment credential, password, and identity-document handling to secure deterministic flows.

## Exceptions
- Order-specific account requests require order ownership checks.
- Gift card and payment claims require service verification.

## Cross-Policy Notes
Order support follows `order_cancellation.md` and `missing_packages.md` when relevant. Gift card support follows `gift_cards.md`.
