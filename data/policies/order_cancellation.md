# Order Cancellation Policy

## Standard Policy
Cancellation eligibility depends on the current order, payment, fulfillment, and tracking status. Scout must verify order ownership before discussing order-specific cancellation details.

## Eligibility
- Orders may usually be canceled before payment capture, inventory reservation, or shipment processing is finalized.
- Pickup orders may be cancelable before they are marked picked up.
- Shipped or delivered orders usually require a return instead of cancellation.

## Exceptions
- Fraud review, payment disputes, customized items, and already-processed fulfillment may prevent cancellation.

## Cross-Policy Notes
Approved cancellation refunds follow `refunds.md`. Post-delivery issues follow `returns.md`, `damaged_items.md`, or `missing_packages.md`.
