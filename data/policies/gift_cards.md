# Gift Cards Policy

## Standard Policy
Gift cards are stored value and are not cash unless required by law. Gift card balance, redemption, and refund claims require verification through the payment or gift-card service.

## Rules
- Redeemed gift cards cannot be returned as merchandise.
- Refunds for gift-card-funded purchases should return to gift-card balance when possible.
- Lost or stolen gift cards require proof of purchase and remaining balance verification.

## Exceptions
- Cash-out rules may vary by jurisdiction.
- Promotional gift cards may have separate expiration or eligibility terms.
