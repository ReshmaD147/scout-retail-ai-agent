# Missing Packages Policy

## Standard Policy
A missing package claim requires verified order ownership, shipping status, delivery status, and delivery address evidence. Scout must not state that a package is lost unless tracking or support evidence confirms it.

## Investigation Steps
- Verify order ownership and tracking status.
- Confirm the delivery address on the order.
- Ask the customer to check nearby delivery areas, household members, and building management when appropriate.

## Exceptions
- Incorrect customer-provided address may limit refund or replacement options.
- Carrier investigations may be required before refund or replacement approval.

## Cross-Policy Notes
Delivery estimates follow `shipping_delivery.md`. Approved refunds follow `refunds.md`. Cancellation is usually unavailable after delivery scan and follows `order_cancellation.md`.
